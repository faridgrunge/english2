Where are you from?

(Anda berasal dari mana? )

 

English

James : Hello.
Jamal : Hi.

James : How are you?
Jamal : I'm good. How are you?

James : Good. Do you speak English?
Jamal : A little. Are you American?

James : Yes.
Jamal : Where are you from?

James : I'm from California.
Jamal : Nice to meet you.

James : Nice to meet you too.
