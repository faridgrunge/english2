Taking Pictures

(Mengambil foto)

 

English

William : Excuse me, sir, will you take a picture of us?
Supardi : Sure. This is a really nice camera.

William : Thanks, my parents gave it to me.
Supardi : How do you use it?

William : You press this button here.
Supardi : Come a little closer. Wait, that's too close. Move a little to the left. Move back a little farther. OK, stay right there.

William : Do you mean here?
Supardi : Yes, that's good. Oh, I can't get it to work.

William : You need to hold down the button for about 3 seconds.
Supardi : OK I got it, are you ready?

William : Yes.
Supardi : Smile.

 

Indonesian

William: Maaf, Pak, tolong ambilkan foto (dengan kamera) kami?
Supardi: Tentu saja. Kamera ini bagus sekali.

William: Terima kasih, orang tua saya yang berikan kamera itu.
Supardi: Bagaimana cara menggunakannya?

William: Anda tekan tombol di sini.
Supardi: Tolong lebih mendekat. Tunggu, itu terlalu dekat. Geser sedikit ke kiri. Geser ke belakang. Oke, tahan di sana.

William: Maksud Anda di sini?
Supardi: Ya, bagus. Oh, saya tidak bisa menjalankan kamera ini.

William: Anda perlu menahan tombol itu selama sekitar tiga detik.
Supardi: Oke mengerti, Anda siap?

William: Ya.
Supardi: Senyum..
