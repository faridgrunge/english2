I am hungry?

(Saya Lapar? )

 

English

Thomas : Hi Dimas, how are you?
Dimas : Fine, how are you doing?

Thomas : OK.
Dimas : What do you want to do?

Thomas : I'm hungry. I'd like to eat something.
Dimas : Where do you want to go?

Thomas : I'd like to go to an Italian restaurant.
Dimas : What kind of Italian food do you like?

Thomas : I like spaghetti. Do you like spaghetti?
Dimas : No, I don't, but I like pizza.

 

Indonesian

Thomas: Hai Dimas, apa kabar?
Dimas: Baik, gimana kabarnya?

Thomas: Oke
Dimas: Mau buat apa?

Thomas: Saya lapar. Saya mau makan sesuatu.
Dimas: Mau pergi ke mana?

Thomas: Saya mau ke restoran Italia
Dimas: Makanan Italia apa yang Anda suka?

Thomas: Saya suka spaghetti. Anda suka spaghetti?
Dimas: Tidak, tapi saya suka pizza.
