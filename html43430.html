Asking Direction?

(Menanyakan Arah? )

 

English

Amy : Hi Ary.
Ary : Hi Amy. What's up?

Amy : I'm looking for the airport. Can you tell me how to get there?
Ary : No, sorry. I don't know.

Amy : I think I can take the subway to the airport. Do you know where the subway is?
Ary : Sure, it's over there.

Amy : Where? I don't see it.
Ary : Across the street.

Amy : Oh, I see it now. Thanks.
Ary : No problem.

Amy : Do you know if there's a restroom around here?
Ary : Yes, there's one here. It's in the store.

Amy : Thank you.
Ary : Bye.

Amy : Bye bye.

 

Indonesian

Amy: Hai Ary
Ary: Hai Amy. Gimana?


Amy: Saya sedang mencari bandara udara. Anda tahu jalan ke sana?
Ary: Tidak tahu, maaf ya.

Amy: Saya kira saya bisa naik subway ke bandara. Anda tahu subway di mana?
Ary: Tentu saja. Itu di sebelah sana.

Amy: Di mana? Saya tidak lihat apa-apa.
Ary: Di seberang jalan.

Amy: Oh, ya, saya lihat sekarang. Terima kasih.
Ary: Sama-sama

Amy: Apakah Anda tahu kalau ada kamar kecil di sekitar sini?
Ary: Ya, ada satu di sana. Ada di dalam toko.

Amy: Terima kasih
Ary: Dah (Sampai nanti)

Amy: Dah (Sampai nanti)
