Get Something to eat

(Mencari Makan? )

 

English

Susy : Mary, would you like to get something to eat with me?
Mary : OK. When?

Susy : At 10 O'clock.
Mary : 10 in the morning?

Susy : No, at night.
Mary : Sorry, that's too late. I usually go to bed around 10:00PM.

Susy : OK, how about 1:30 PM?
Mary : No, that's too early. I'll still be at work then.

Susy : How about 5:00PM?
Mary : That's fine.

Susy : OK, see you then.
Mary : Alright. Bye.

 

Indonesian

Susy: Mary, apakah Anda mau makan dengan saya?
Mary: Oke. Kapan?

Susy: Jam sepuluh.
Mary: Sepuluh pagi?

Susy: Bukan, waktu malam.
Mary: Maaf, itu terlalu malam. Saya biasanya tidur sekitar jam sepuluh malam.

Susy: Oke, bagaimana kalau jam setengah dua siang?
Mary: Tidak bisa, itu terlalu awal. Saya masih kerja pada jam itu.

Susy: Bagaimana kalau jam lima sore?
Mary: Baiklah.

Susy: Oke, sampai bertemu nanti.
Mary: Baiklah. Dah!
