Past Future Continuous Tense

Pengertian:

Past future continuous tense adalah suatu bentuk kata kerja untuk menyatakan aksi atau situasi imajiner yang sedang berlangsung apabila unreal condition-nya terpenuhi (present continuous conditional ~ conditional sentence type 2 dengan continuous tense).

Rumus:

Jenis Kalimat Rumus Contoh
positif
(+)		 S + would be + V1-ing/present participle He would be working
We would be studying
negatif
(-)		 S + would + not + be + V1-ing/present participle He wouldn’t be working
We wouldn’t be studying
interogatif
(?)		 Would + S + be + V1-ing/present participle Would he be working
Would we be studying

 

 

Contoh Kalimat Past Future Continuous Tense

Contoh Kalimat Kenyataannya (Fact)
I would be attending the conference if I was in Jakarta.
(Saya akan sedang menghadiri konferensi tersebut jika saya ada di Jakarta.)		 but I’m not in Jakarta  (tapi saya tidak di Jakarta)
We would be doing some soil tests if you brought soil samples.
(Kita akan sedang melakuan beberapa tes tanah jika kamu membawa contoh tanah.)		 but you doesn’t bring soil samples (tapi kamu tidak membawa contoh tanah)
Would you be having breakfast if you had at least 2 slices of bread?
(Apakah kamu akan sedang sarapan kalau kamu punya setidaknya dua potong roti?)		 but you have no bread (tapi kamu tidak punya roti)
If she came, the show would be taking place smoothly.
