A dialogue between yourself and a foreigner who has come to Bangladesh for the first time.

Myself : Hello, Im Tamal. Are you here for the first time?
Foreigner :Hello, Im Tanaka from Japan. This is my first visit to Bangladesh.

Myself : Can I help you?
Foreigner : Yes, at first I want to go to Coxs Bazer, the longest sea-beach in the world. And then to other beautiful places. Will you please guide me?

Myself :Of course, How do you like to go, by bus, by train or by air?
Foreigner :Id like to go by bus.

Myself :O. k, Ill make an arrangement to go there. Where will you stay this night?
Foreigner : Oh, I want to rest the night in any good hotel in Dhaka.

Myself : Here are the addresses of some aristocratic hotels in Dhaka.
Foreigner : Thank you very much.
